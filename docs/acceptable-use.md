You may not:

* Use bots, scripts, automation tools, or headless browsers

---

* Use the Service to develop, test, benchmark, or improve a competing product

---

### 2.3 API and System Abuse

* Access backend APIs outside of the authorized Extension
* Attempt to bypass authentication or subscription controls
